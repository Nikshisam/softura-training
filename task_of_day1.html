Easy lunch for your busy weak days


Do just 6 steps and get ready for your day's work

What are the things needed?




    1. Boiled rice








    2. Lemon








    3. Mustard seeds








    4. Urad dal








    5. Gram








    6. Red chilly








    7. Curry leaves








    8. Turmeric powder








      9. How to do?





      Step 1 : Take a bowl and squeeze a lemon in it (amount of lemon deponds upon the amount of rice to be cooked).


      Step 2 : Rice in a required amount should be boiled priorly before the making of dish.


      Step 3 : Keep a pan on the stove and pour some oil in it.


      Step 4 : After the oil gets heated put some mustard seeds ,urad dal,gram flour,red chilli and curry leaves in it .


      Step 5 : Fry it together until it gets some golden brown color .&npsp After that pour the lemon juice which has been kept squeezed add some amount of turmeric powder .


      Step 6 : Finally add the boiled rice to the pan .     Add needed amount of salt




      Try it and make your lunch without any rush up in the morning
